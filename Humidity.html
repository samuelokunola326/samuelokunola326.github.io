Latitude vs Humidity

Analysis

This visualization compares the Humidity percentage by the latitude of the city. Based on what we are seeing it looks like for the most part latitude has little to no effect on the Humidity percentage. The only exception worth noting is that in locations below -60 degrees latitude seem to have little to no cloudiness this could be a result of the limitation of our data size as well and would need further review. In general it seems the Humidity will likely be between 60% and 100% for most cities visited on a given day.
