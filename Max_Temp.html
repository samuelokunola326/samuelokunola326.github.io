Latitude vs Max Tempurature

Analysis

This visualization compares the max tempurature by the latitude of the city. Based on what we are seeing it looks like latitude has a strong correlation on the tempurature of a given city. Cities closes to the equator have the highest tempuratures on average, with cities more than 40 degrees in either direction of the equator having a tempurature below 50 degrees on average. Suprisingly, the western hemiphere is home to most of the extremely cold cities with a handle full having tempuratures below 0.
