Latitude vs Wind Speed

Analysis

This visualization compares the wind speed(MPH) by the latitude of the city. Based on what we are seeing it looks like for the most part latitude has a small effect on the wind speed(MPH). With cities in the eastern hemiphere of the world reporting higher wind speeds, with a handful of cities having extreme winds(above 20mph).
